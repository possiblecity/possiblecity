About

Possible City is a collaborative platform that connects people, imagination, skills, and collective resources to transform abandoned spaces into generative places.

We're building community dialogue around abandoned spaces with a focus on confronting the digital divide, by posting physical signage on vacant lots to prompt ideas from residents via text, tweet and write-in paper form. At the same time we're engaging artists, designers, urban farmers and small business people to float ideas of their own - and helping them find the constituencies, resources, and people they need to get their projects on the ground.
